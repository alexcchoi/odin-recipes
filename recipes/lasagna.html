Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a hearty and comforting Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta noodles with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella.

  • 9 lasagna noodles (cooked)
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz / 680g) tomato pasta sauce
  • 1 container (15 oz / 425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. To make lasagna, start by cooking ground beef in a pan until it's browned, then add tomato sauce and let it simmer.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg and some seasoning.
  3. In a baking dish, layer cooked lasagna noodles, the meat sauce, the ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  4. Repeat the layers until the dish is full, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  5. Bake it in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 45 minutes, then let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
